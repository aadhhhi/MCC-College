Institute Overview

Your Local Trusted Training Institute in Thrissur

MCC College (also known as MCC Computers) is a job-oriented training institute located at Sakthan Stand, Thrissur. We specialize in computer courses, professional accounting programs, teacher training, and university degree support — all taught by experienced faculty with a focus on practical, employable skills.

We are a C-DIT (Centre for Development of Imaging Technology) affiliated institute, recognized by the Government of Kerala.
